Story / यात्रा

From grassroots organizing to international collaboration, NB Tandan’s journey is about connecting people with purpose — celebrating Nepali identity while forging lasting ties with Canadian communities.

Early
Mobilized Nepali diaspora networks, promoting cultural pride and mutual support.
Growth
Partnered with organizations and cities to showcase Nepal’s tourism and heritage.
Today
Leading initiatives that elevate trade, education, and cultural exchange between Nepal and Canada.
