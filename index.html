About

NB Tandan is an entrepreneur, tourism enthusiast, social activist and president of Canada Nepal Tourism Culture and Trade based in Canada. His business is mainly focused in tourism and transportation industries.

Uniting Nepal and Canada Since the Beginning

Bridging cultures, communities, and opportunities through dedication and vision.

From fostering tourism to promoting cultural exchange, NB Tandan has been instrumental in strengthening the bond between Nepal and Canada. His journey reflects the power of unity and grassroots action across borders.

Resume

What We Do

Under the leadership of N B Tandan, our mission is to build lasting cultural and economic bridges between Nepal and Canada through advocacy, events, and strategic initiatives.

Tourism Promotion

Representing Nepal as a Goodwill Ambassador and promoting sustainable tourism between Canada and Nepal through CNTCT events and outreach campaigns.

Cultural Exchange

Organizing multicultural festivals, language programs, and artistic showcases to foster appreciation of Nepali heritage in Canada and globally.

Community Advocacy

Engaging with Canadian institutions to uplift the South Asian diaspora and provide platforms for youth, entrepreneurs, and cultural leaders.

Trade & Investment

Facilitating trade dialogue, import/export guidance, and partnership development between Nepali and Canadian businesses through CNTCT initiatives.

Humanitarian Relief

Supporting urgent causes such as flood relief and disaster recovery in Nepal, with grassroots and diaspora-driven mobilization.

Public Representation

Representing Nepal at global forums including the World Leaders Summit, advocating for culture, tourism, and inclusive development worldwide.
